Why 3D Scanning Changes Everything

SEE YOUR ASSET
IN FULL DIMENSION

Imagine handing a client, architect, engineer, or planning officer a fully interactive, measurable 3D model of your building, site, or asset — captured in a single day, from the air, with centimetre-level accuracy. That is exactly what OverSky delivers.

Our drone photogrammetry service turns hundreds of overlapping aerial images into rich, textured 3D models you can rotate, measure, annotate, and share. No scaffolding, no shutdowns, no guesswork. Whether you are a property developer, heritage trust, engineering firm, or insurance assessor, a 3D scan from OverSky gives you an asset record that lasts forever and pays for itself immediately.

The Process

HOW IT
WORKS

01🗺️

Site Assessment & Planning

We assess your site remotely and on the ground, plan the optimal flight grid, and handle all airspace authorisation before we arrive.

02📸

Aerial Capture

Hundreds of precisely timed, geo-tagged images captured at calibrated altitudes — overlapping to within millimetre tolerances for maximum mesh quality.

03⚙️

Photogrammetry Processing

Images are processed through professional software to produce a fully textured, measurable 3D model in your chosen format.

Delivery & Sharing
